# Online-Book-Store-
Online Book Store Management System | SQL Project

Project Description

. Designed and developed an Online Book Store Management System using SQL to manage and analyze bookstore data efficiently.
. Created and worked on multiple relational database tables including Books, Customers, and Orders.
. Performed data analysis to generate meaningful business insights related to sales, inventory, customers, and book categories.
. Implemented SQL queries to retrieve, filter, sort, and analyze bookstore records.
. Analyzed customer purchasing patterns, book demand, and revenue generation.
. Calculated inventory stock and identified books with low availability.
. Generated reports for genre-wise sales, customer orders, and total revenue.
. Used aggregate functions, joins, subqueries, and grouping operations for advanced data analysis.
. Improved database querying and problem-solving skills through real-world business scenarios.
. Demonstrated practical understanding of database management and SQL-based business analytics.
